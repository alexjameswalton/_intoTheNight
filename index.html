Into The Night



Create a night to remember with some of the south coast's leading amateur musicians. Into-The-Night is a high class soul, funk & pop band ready to make your event - wedding, birthday, function, corporate - an absolute banger. We play dancefloor classics from every decade and all the stars, from Mercury to Mars.


Sam

A regular feature on the south-coast scene, soul-sister Sam has performed at national venues including the London Palladium and Southampton Mayflower, and supported premier acts like Toploader.


Jack

Our guitar-hero is a successful artist in his own right, with his music featured multiple times on BBC Introducing. Jack has supported stars such as Tom Jones and Lemar, and played venues including the O2 and Wembley Arena.


Alex

Recently back from India, including gigs with Sanjeev Thomas & Keith Peters (Slumdog Millionaire), Alex is the band's newest addition. His career highlights include Glastonbury Festival and Montreal Jazz Fest.


Chris

Bass-man Chris is a talented multi-instrumentalist, able to play anything with strings. He gigs with top UK tribute acts, has toured the UK, France and Spain, and performed at top venues including Royal Albert Hall.


Dave

Dave established himself in the northern rock scene, regularly gigging across Sheffield and Manchester including at the famous Boardwalk. He now lays down some of the smoothest grooves on the south-coast.
